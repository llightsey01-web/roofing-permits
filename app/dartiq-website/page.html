How It Works

From job details to approved permit

Six automated steps. Your team never touches a county portal.

01

Submit job details

Enter the property address and scope. DART iQ handles everything from there.

02

Parcel & legal description

Parcel data and legal descriptions are pulled automatically from county records.

03

NOC generated & signed

Notice of Commencement is generated and sent to the homeowner for remote notarization.

04

NOC recorded

The notarized NOC is eRecorded with the county clerk — no courthouse trips.

05

Permit submitted

DART iQ navigates the AHJ portal, submits the application, and tracks approval status.

06

Permit packet ready

Your complete permit packet — all documents in one PDF — ready for the job site.
