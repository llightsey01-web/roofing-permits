How It Works

From job details to approved permit

Six automated steps. You never touch a portal.

Step 1
Submit job details

Enter the property address and job scope. DART iQ handles everything from there.

Step 2
Parcel & legal description retrieved

DART iQ automatically pulls parcel data and legal descriptions from county records.

Step 3
NOC generated & sent for signing

Notice of Commencement is generated and sent to the homeowner for remote digital notarization.

Step 4
NOC recorded with county

The notarized NOC is eRecorded with the county clerk automatically — no trips to the courthouse.

Step 5
Permit submitted & approved

DART iQ navigates the AHJ portal, submits the permit application, and tracks approval status in real time.

Step 6
Permit Packet Ready

Your complete permit packet — all documents compiled into one PDF — ready to print and bring to the job site.
